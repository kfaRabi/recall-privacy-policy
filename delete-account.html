Account Deletion Information

Our app uses Google Sign-In (OAuth) via Supabase for authentication. We do not store any personal data beyond what Supabase requires for login functionality.

Important: Your vocabulary progress, learning data, and preferences are stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this information on our servers.

How to delete your account:

  • Send us a deletion request via email
  • We will delete your Supabase authentication entry
  • This removes your login session and account completely

What data is deleted:

  • Supabase session data (used for login authentication)
  • Email address (stored by Supabase for authentication)
  • Basic profile information (display name, profile picture from Google)

What is NOT affected:

  • No progress or analytics data is stored on our servers
  • Your local learning data remains on your device until you uninstall the app

Data Retention: We do not retain any user data after account deletion. The process is permanent and cannot be undone.
