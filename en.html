Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29.05.2025

Thank you for using Recall: German Vocabulary (“the App”). Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

We only collect and use the minimal information required to provide our service:

  • Authentication Data: When you sign in using Google, we use Supabase to authenticate you. Supabase stores:
    • Your email address
    • Basic profile information (such as your display name and profile picture)
    • Timestamps of account creation and last login

    We do not store any of this information ourselves. Supabase handles it securely on your behalf. Supabase acts as a third-party data processor for this authentication data. You can review Supabase's privacy policy here: [Link to Supabase Privacy Policy].

  • Local Device Data: Your learning progress (words learned, deck usage, streaks) is stored only on your device. We do not have access to this data.
  • Ads: We use non-personalized ads to support the app. These ads do not track you or create a profile.

2. How Do We Use Your Information?

We use the data described above for the following purposes:

  • To enable sign-in via Google
  • To manage your session using Supabase Auth
  • To serve non-personalized advertisements

3. Do We Share Your Information?

No. We do not share, sell, or transfer any personal data. Supabase handles all authentication securely and independently.

4. Cookies or Tracking

We do not use cookies or analytics tracking.

5. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data beyond what Supabase handles during active sessions.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to individuals under the age of 13 (or a higher age threshold as required by applicable law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age. If we become aware that a child under this age has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with information, please contact us at [hellodigitalomen@proton.me].

7. Your Rights (Under GDPR)

As a user in the EU, you have the right to:

  • Access your authentication data (via Supabase)
  • Request correction or deletion (handled through your Google account)
  • Withdraw consent at any time (by signing out)

You may contact us at [hellodigitalomen@proton.me] for privacy-related requests.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
